My Identity, Values and Strengths.

What are my values?

My main values would include:

  • Integrity - to me, this comes into being true to yourself and owning up.
  • Ownership - I believe having ownership creates good and honest character.
  • Responsibility - I sort of see responsibility and organisation going hand-in-hand, as well as discipline. Sometimes responsibility requires organisation and discipline, so it's necessary to possess those attributes sometimes.
  • Gratitude - being thankful for what you have. In my experience, no matter how bad things can get, if you sit down and think about what you are grateful for, sometimes it can make life a little less dim.
